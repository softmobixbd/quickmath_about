Welcome to QuickMath Quest!

At SoftMobiXBD, we believe that learning should be both fun and engaging. With the increasing use of mobile devices among young users, we wanted to create something educational and entertaining—a game that transforms screen time into a productive learning experience.

Why We Created QuickMath Quest

Many young people spend hours on mobile devices, often engaging with content that doesn’t contribute to their growth. That’s why we developed QuickMath Quest—a fun and interactive way to sharpen math skills while enjoying the challenge of a game. Whether you love numbers or want to improve your math-solving skills, this app provides an enjoyable way to practice addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Who Can Play?

QuickMath Quest is designed for students from 13 to 17 years old (Grades 8 to 12). However, it’s suitable for anyone who enjoys math challenges—even adults looking to test their speed and accuracy!

What Makes Our App Special?

Unlike many other math games, QuickMath Quest offers fully randomized questions, ensuring that each play-through is unique. The app includes multiple exciting game modes:

  • ✅ Simple Math – Solve problems at your own pace
  • ✅ Time Challenge – Answer as many questions as possible in a set time (10–60 seconds)
  • ✅ True or False – Quickly decide if the math statement is correct
  • ✅ Drag & Match – Move the correct answer to the question
  • ✅ Draw to Match – Connect the right answer with a line

These diverse game styles make math practice more engaging and dynamic!
