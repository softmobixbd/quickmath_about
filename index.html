Welcome to the Quest!

At SoftMobiXBD, we believe that education should be highly interactive and entertaining. In an era where mobile devices dominate modern screen time, our goal was to engineer a platform that shifts casual play into a productive, cognitive training routine.

Why QuickMath Quest?

Many digital spaces engage users without stimulating brain growth. QuickMath Quest bridges that gap by functioning as a high-octane mental workspace disguised as an exciting puzzle game. Whether you are looking to master basic arithmetic speed or tackle logic grids, the application scales dynamically to refine your speed, accuracy, and working memory.

Who Is It For?

While fine-tuned with progression targets ideal for students aged 13 to 17 (Grades 8 to 12), the game features flexible difficulty architecture. It is an optimized fit for puzzle enthusiasts of all ages looking to defend their logical edge and test operational boundaries.

The Complete Puzzle Suite

Unlike static arithmetic drills, QuickMath Quest utilizes completely randomized equation generation across 8 unique training modules to ensure no two sessions feel the same:

  • 📊 Simple Math Solve fundamental operations at a comfortable, non-timed training pace.
  • ⏳ Time Challenge Race against a ticking clock across custom interval rounds ranging from 10 to 60 seconds.
  • ✅ True or False Execute split-second validation checks on complex statements under pressure.
  • 🎯 Drag & Match Physically rearrange structural equations to target precise answers.
  • 📈 Draw to Match Trace paths dynamically across the grid to link compatible values instantly.
  • 🔍 Find X, Y, Z An advanced algebra block dedicated to isolating hidden variables and cracking algebraic expressions.
  • 🧩 Strategic Sudoku Train logic and structural placement utilizing adaptive, beginner-friendly Sudoku matrices.
  • 🔢 2028 Challenge A tactical grid game focused on sliding, stacking, and merging tiles to maximize numerical high scores.
